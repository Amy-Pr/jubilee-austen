Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Portland, Oregon, with my beloved Italian greyhound. My journey into tech started as a proofreader and copyeditor for digital media groups. As I learned the ins and outs of the editorial process, I worked closely with web developers and content management systems to make the editorial team's visions into a reality. I began to learn the basics of code, and was hooked ever since.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to create and customize my first WordPress blog, I realized I found something that gave me new creativity and versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I hope to make a switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
